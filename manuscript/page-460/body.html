Then they told him the adventures of the cat, and showed him the cabinet of jewels which they had brought for Mr. Whittington. Upon which he cried out with great earnestness, but not in the most poetical manner:

“Go, send him in, and tell him of his fame, And call him Mr. Whittington by name.”

It is not our business to animadvert upon these lines; we are not critics, but historians.
